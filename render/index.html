One play
This is real art.
I play a piece of my music while recording the audio directly to the cassette.
I then instal a breaking mechanism inside, so the tape destroys as being played.
Tape is sealed, no rewind.
I have few meaningful moments with this piece. May the listener too.
This is as real as life, this is art.
Any tape in any conditions.
